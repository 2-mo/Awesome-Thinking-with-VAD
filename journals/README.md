# Top-Tier Journal Papers



## 2025

Top-tier papers in 2025 revolve around reasoning-oriented and privacy-aware VAD, highlighting multilingual prompt-guided feature learning, causal consistency modeling, and low-latency anonymization that edge the field closer to real-world deployment.

#### Scene-Dependent Prediction in Latent Space for Video Anomaly Detection and Anticipation
[![TPAMI](https://img.shields.io/badge/TPAMI-2025-0B3D91)](https://ieeexplore.ieee.org/document/10681297)
> Learns scene-aware latent dynamics to detect and anticipate anomalies before they unfold.

#### Multilingual-Prompt-Guided Directional Feature Learning for Weakly Supervised Video Anomaly Detection
[![TPAMI](https://img.shields.io/badge/TPAMI-2025-0B3D91)](https://ieeexplore.ieee.org/document/11082577)
> Uses multilingual prompts to steer directional feature learning under weak supervision.

#### Where and What: Contextual Dynamics-Aware Anomaly Detection in Surveillance Videos
[![TIP](https://img.shields.io/badge/TIP-2025-2D8659)](https://ieeexplore.ieee.org/document/11217333)
> Models contextual dynamics to determine both the location and semantics of anomalies.

#### Dual-Space Normalizing Flow for Unsupervised Video Anomaly Detection
[![TIP](https://img.shields.io/badge/TIP-2025-2D8659)](https://ieeexplore.ieee.org/document/11185330)
> Introduces dual-space normalizing flows to capture normal patterns for unsupervised detection.

#### CRCL: Causal Representation Consistency Learning for Anomaly Detection in Surveillance Videos
[![TIP](https://img.shields.io/badge/TIP-2025-2D8659)](https://ieeexplore.ieee.org/document/10962292)
> Enforces causal consistency across representations to robustly flag surveillance anomalies.

#### PromptVAD: Abnormal Prompt via Vision-Language Model
[![TNNLS](https://img.shields.io/badge/TNNLS-2025-F39C12)](https://ieeexplore.ieee.org/document/11222791)
> Leverages vision-language prompts to surface abnormal events without extensive labels.

#### Privacy-Preserving Video Anomaly Detection: A Survey
[![TNNLS](https://img.shields.io/badge/TNNLS-2025-F39C12)](https://ieeexplore.ieee.org/document/11147183)
> Surveys privacy-aware strategies for building trustworthy VAD systems.

#### Dynamic Erasing Network With Adaptive Temporal Modeling for Weakly Supervised Video Anomaly Detection
[![TNNLS](https://img.shields.io/badge/TNNLS-2025-F39C12)](https://ieeexplore.ieee.org/document/10957754)
> Uses adaptive temporal erasing to refine weakly supervised anomaly localization.

#### Low-Latency Video Anonymization for Crowd Anomaly Detection: Privacy vs. Performance
[![TIFS](https://img.shields.io/badge/TIFS-2025-C0392B)](https://ieeexplore.ieee.org/document/11231379)
> Balances privacy protection and detection accuracy for low-latency crowd monitoring.

## 2024

In 2024, the focus shifted to closing the loop from detection to downstream usage: hybrid frameworks, prompt-augmented context modeling, and crowd behavior analytics strengthened weak supervision and retrieval workflows.

#### Self-Supervised Masked Convolutional Transformer Block for Anomaly Detection
[![TPAMI](https://img.shields.io/badge/TPAMI-2024-0B3D91)](https://ieeexplore.ieee.org/document/10273635)
> Blends masked convolutional and transformer blocks to learn anomaly cues self-supervisedly.

#### Advancing Video Anomaly Detection: A Bi-Directional Hybrid Framework for Enhanced Single- and Multi-Task Approaches
[![TIP](https://img.shields.io/badge/TIP-2024-2D8659)](https://ieeexplore.ieee.org/document/10794594)
> Proposes a bi-directional hybrid that unifies single- and multi-task anomaly objectives.

#### Learning Prompt-Enhanced Context Features for Weakly-Supervised Video Anomaly Detection
[![TIP](https://img.shields.io/badge/TIP-2024-2D8659)](https://ieeexplore.ieee.org/document/10667004)
> Injects prompt-enhanced contextual information to strengthen weakly supervised detection.

#### Toward Video Anomaly Retrieval From Video Anomaly Detection: New Benchmarks and Model
[![TIP](https://img.shields.io/badge/TIP-2024-2D8659)](https://ieeexplore.ieee.org/document/10471334)
> Links anomaly detection outputs to retrieval tasks with new benchmarks and models.

#### Context Recovery and Knowledge Retrieval: A Novel Two-Stream Framework for Video Anomaly Detection
[![TIP](https://img.shields.io/badge/TIP-2024-2D8659)](https://ieeexplore.ieee.org/document/10462921)
> Deploys a two-stream design to recover context while retrieving knowledge for detection.

#### Clustering Aided Weakly Supervised Training to Detect Anomalous Events in Surveillance Videos
[![TNNLS](https://img.shields.io/badge/TNNLS-2024-F39C12)](https://ieeexplore.ieee.org/document/10136845)
> Guides weak supervision with clustering signals to highlight anomalous surveillance events.

#### Distilling Privileged Knowledge for Anomalous Event Detection From Weakly Labeled Videos
[![TNNLS](https://img.shields.io/badge/TNNLS-2024-F39C12)](https://ieeexplore.ieee.org/document/10098140)

#### Weakly Supervised Video Anomaly Detection via Self-Guided Temporal Discriminative Transformer
[![TCYB](https://img.shields.io/badge/TCYB-2024-8E44AD)](https://ieeexplore.ieee.org/document/10002867)

#### Detecting and Quantifying Crowd-Level Abnormal Behaviors in Crowd Events
[![TIFS](https://img.shields.io/badge/TIFS-2024-C0392B)](https://ieeexplore.ieee.org/document/10584564)

## 2023

Throughout 2023, research laid the groundwork with unsupervised traffic monitoring, generative predictive modeling, and large-scale benchmark curation, establishing the data and model base for later reasoning-centric approaches.

#### DoTA: Unsupervised Detection of Traffic Anomaly in Driving Videos
[![TPAMI](https://img.shields.io/badge/TPAMI-2023-0B3D91)](https://ieeexplore.ieee.org/document/9712446)
> Detects traffic anomalies in driving footage without supervised labels.

#### A Kalman Variational Autoencoder Model Assisted by Odometric Clustering for Video Frame Prediction and Anomaly Detection
[![TIP](https://img.shields.io/badge/TIP-2023-2D8659)](https://ieeexplore.ieee.org/document/9991021)
> Combines Kalman VAEs with odometric clustering for prediction-driven anomaly detection.

#### Self-Supervised Attentive Generative Adversarial Networks for Video Anomaly Detection
[![TNNLS](https://img.shields.io/badge/TNNLS-2023-F39C12)](https://ieeexplore.ieee.org/document/9749781)
> Employs attentive GANs in a self-supervised loop to model normality.

#### Video Anomaly Detection via Visual Cloze Tests
[![TIFS](https://img.shields.io/badge/TIFS-2023-C0392B)](https://ieeexplore.ieee.org/document/10197574)
> Frames anomaly detection as a visual cloze test to capture temporal irregularities.

#### Decouple and Resolve: Transformer-Based Models for Online Anomaly Detection From Weakly Labeled Videos
[![TIFS](https://img.shields.io/badge/TIFS-2023-C0392B)](https://ieeexplore.ieee.org/document/9926133)
> Uses transformer decoupling to handle online detection with weak labels.

#### HiEve: A Large-Scale Benchmark for Human-Centric Video Analysis in Complex Events
[![IJCV](https://img.shields.io/badge/IJCV-2023-2C3E50)](https://link.springer.com/article/10.1007/s11263-023-01842-6)
> Delivers the HiEve benchmark for complex, human-centric event understanding.
